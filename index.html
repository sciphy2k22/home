About

Sci-Phy is an inter-college quiz competition event organised by the Department of Physics, Ramakrishna Mission Residential College (Autonomous), Narendrapur every year. However due to recent pandemic situation we could not arrange the event. But this year on 7th May we are coming back with more enthusiasm than ever. This year we are celebrating Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav as our theme and promoting the underrated contribution of Indian Physicists. We are arranging the whole programme in the following two segments.

Quiz

This is Inter-College Quiz Competition where the questions will be based on +2 level physics and basic undergraduate level physics requiring little bit intuition.

Seminar

There will be a exciting on Higgs Boson which will be deliverd by Prof. Soumitra Sengupta, IACS, Kolkata
