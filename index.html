About

Sci-Phy is an inter-college quiz event organised by the Department of Physics, Ramakrishna Mission Residential College (Autonomous), Narendrapur. Due to recent pandemic situation, we could not arrange the event for the last two years. But this year on 7th May we are coming back with more enthusiasm than ever. The theme of our event this year is ‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’, as we celebrate 75 years of India’s independence, and the groundbreaking achievements of our country’s brilliant physicists. We are arranging the whole programme in the following segments.

Quiz

An Inter-College Quiz Competition on Physics

Seminar

Talk on Higgs Boson by Prof. Soumitra Sengupta, IACS, Kolkata

Scientilla

Wall Magazine published by the Department of Physics, RKMRC
