Seminar

The Higgs Boson has been the talk of modern physics for the recent years. With more insight and information on the topic, we have Prof. Soumitra Sengupta delivering a talk on the very day of our quiz event.

Prof. Sengupta is a distinguished academician and is the Amal Kumar Raychaudhuri Chair Professor in the School of Physical Sciences at The Indian Association for the Cultivation of Science. He is a senior professor in the Department of Theoretical Physics and his topics of interest are Extra-dimensional Theories, String Theory, Supersymmetry, Supergravity, Braneworld Phenomenology Cosmology and Blackholes.
